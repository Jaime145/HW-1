Altun Ha



As a result of my illness and the fact that I couldn't spend long outside in the sun without passing out, Altun Ha was dull for me.
It was going to be my last opportunity to visit my grandfather, so I was looking forward to it. I feel a bit sad whenever I go to Altun Ha because it reminds me so much of him.
It's interesting to me that there have been no stelae or other glyphic inscriptions found at the site that chronicle the names of its kings or connections to other Maya sites..
