THE BELIZE ZOO



I've been to the Belize Zoo so much that I'm starting to become a little bored with it.
When I was 10, 14, and 16, I visited. My parents wanted to demonstrate my siblings the various kinds of creatures that live in our environment and the need for us to protect them, so that's why we went. Which is sort of the justification for my want to own a pet tiger, lol.
For once, I'm happy to see Belize conserving its wild species by establishing reserves and enacting legislation to do so. Belize has a lot to safeguard, but few people are aware of it.
