The Great Blue Hole



One of my favorite places I have ever been was the Blue Hole. Due to my phobia of sharks and other aquatic creatures, snorkeling made me quite nervous. I made an effort to enjoy the snorkeling a little bit.
We got a distant glimpse of some sharks. I took a little dive under the boat, which was extremely neat.
The Belize Blue Hole is one of the seven natural wonders of the world, I have learned. Which is fantastic for me as Belize has so many lovely things.
