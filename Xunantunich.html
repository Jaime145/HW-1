Xunantunich



How was it?

I went with my family to Xunantunich when I was about 8 or 9 years old. An outing as a family is something my parents want. Being able to witness a Maya Temple for the first time made me happy. I'm missing out, according to what my pals have always said to me. The moment I arrived there, I was awestruck by its beauty.
The forestry just awed me, and I learned so much about the area. Did you know that they discovered a figure grasping at ropes while dancing? These ropes are known as birth ropes because they hang from a house beam and are held by women when they give birth.
